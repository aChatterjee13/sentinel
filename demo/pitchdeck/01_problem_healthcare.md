# Slide 01 — The problem (healthcare)

## The two questions every clinician asks

1. **"How will we know when the AI is wrong?"**
2. **"Who is accountable when it is?"**

## And the three you haven't been asked yet

3. How do we prove PHI never left our tenant?
4. How do we prove the agent cannot write to the EHR?
5. How do we replay a reasoning trace for a medical-legal review six months
   from now?

### The honest answer today

"We'll figure that out before go-live."

That answer stops pilots from becoming production.

---

*Speaker note:* Don't oversell. Healthcare buyers respect caution, not
swagger. Let them see you as the one vendor who isn't pretending the hard
questions don't exist.
