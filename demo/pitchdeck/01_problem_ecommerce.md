# Slide 01 — The problem (e-commerce)

## NDCG is going up. Revenue is going down. Why?

### The silent killers of recommender quality

| Failure mode | What it looks like | What your vendor shows |
|---|---|---|
| Long-tail collapse | Recs concentrate on top-1% of items | NDCG up |
| Popularity bias runaway | Gini of impression share → 0.95 | NDCG up |
| Cold-start starvation | New products get zero impressions | NDCG up |
| Segment fairness gap | Loyal users get better recs than new users | NDCG up |
| Diversity drop | Every recommendation list looks the same | NDCG up |

### The question you don't have an answer to

> "Is our recommender actually surfacing the full catalogue, or is it
> quietly funnelling everyone to the same 50 products?"
