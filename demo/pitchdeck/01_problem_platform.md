# Slide 01 — The problem (enterprise agent platform)

## Your agent estate, honestly

- **3 frameworks** (LangGraph, Semantic Kernel, CrewAI / ADK)
- **10–30 agents** in prod or on the way
- **4+ teams** each with their own monitoring approach
- **1 runaway incident** last month that nobody could kill from outside
  the process
- **1 mystery LLM bill** this quarter that nobody can attribute to a team

### The three things your platform team cannot do today

1. Enforce a tool permission policy across every framework.
2. Attribute cost to an agent, prompt version, and team in one query.
3. Show the auditor the full reasoning trace for a specific agent action.

### The operating model you'd like

> One policy. One config. Every framework. Every agent. Every safety rail.

---

*Speaker note:* Platform teams respond to the word "consolidation". They are
drowning in vendors and they are the ones who will champion you internally.
