# Slide 01 — The problem (UK bank)

## How many tools are in your ML governance stack right now?

- **Evidently** for drift
- **MLflow** for the registry
- **Custom Python** for alerting
- **Azure DevOps YAML** for deployment
- **A spreadsheet** for model inventory
- **Another spreadsheet** for SS1/23 evidence
- **Slack threads** for the audit trail

### The cost

- 6–8 weeks of plumbing per new model
- Audit prep = 2 weeks of evidence-gathering, every cycle
- Canary rollouts are hand-run, rollbacks are hand-run
- Nobody has a single source of truth for "which model is in production"

---

*Speaker note:* Let them nod. Ask *"which of those would you like to
consolidate?"* and listen. Whatever they say first is the act you lean into
in the demo.
